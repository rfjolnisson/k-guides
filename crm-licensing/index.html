While Salesforce provides standard CRM objects like Leads, Opportunities, and Cases, Kaptio extends the platform with purpose-built objects designed specifically for multi-day travel operations:

Trip Object: Acts as the overarching container for customer intent throughout the entire journey. A Trip captures the customer's interest from the very first inquiry and tracks it through distinct lifecycle stages:

  • Prospecting & Qualification (initial interest)
  • Quote Pending & Quote Sent (proposal stage)
  • Confirmed & Travelling (booking fulfilled)
  • Completed (post-trip)

Itinerary Object: Represents specific configurations and proposals within a Trip. You can create multiple Itineraries for a single Trip—each with different dates, services, pricing, and options—allowing customers to compare alternatives.

Marketing Integration: Because the Trip object tracks the complete customer lifecycle, it becomes incredibly powerful for marketing automation:

  • Trigger automated follow-ups based on Trip status
  • Re-engage customers with expired quotes
  • Send post-trip surveys and upsell opportunities
  • Segment customers by travel stage for targeted campaigns
  • Integrate with marketing automation platforms (Marketo, Pardot, HubSpot, etc.)

While Salesforce Sales Cloud provides general-purpose Leads and Opportunities for any industry, Kaptio's Trip and Itinerary objects are purpose-built for the unique complexity of multi-day travel operations. This industry-specific approach provides a stronger foundation for travel businesses and integrates naturally with Salesforce's Service Cloud and Marketing Cloud as you scale.
